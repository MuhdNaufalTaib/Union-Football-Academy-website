About UFA

Union Football Academy where the world's best footballers are made here! The leading football training academy in Asia. Nuturing talents and harnessing their potential. Most of our players get signed by top clubs in Europe which is considered rare and prestigious in Asia.

Our team competes across 2 leagues, and 1 related cup competitions; the AFC Champions League, the World Youth League and also the Asia Cup.

Union Football Academy is committed to providing the best environment for footballers who seek a greater challenge. We would equip every player with the skills and technique to become better footballers. We also have experienced coaches who had played at the top level to drill in the mentality so that the players are prepared for the pro-world. We ensure that our core values(Passion, Teamwork, Responsibilty) is intstilled into every player. We hope that our players not only be the best footballers but also the best in character.

Our Coaches

Coach George

George was a former Arsenal player. He holds an AFC ‘A’ Coaching Certificate and has also completed FIFA Grassroots Coaching, FIFA Fitness Coaching and FIFA Elite Youth Coaching courses.

Coach Lu

Lu joined UFA from Valencia CF as the director there. He holds an AFC ‘A’ Coaching Certificate and FIFA Coaching Certificate, FIFA Physio Coaching and World Youth Coaching courses.

Coach Jose

Jose has played an integral role in the Youth System at UFA. UFA was his first club and became his home. He holds a FIFA Coaching Certificate, FIFA Management Certificate and World Youth Coaching Certificate.

Coach Auba

Auba is a well known face across Singaporean football. Having played in non-league football in France, Auba joined Singapore Premier League team Marina Yishun in 2013. He is on the verge of receiving the Fifa Coaching Certificate.
